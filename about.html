The EduTable is a groundbreaking interactive table designed to provide users with instant access to educational information about various items placedon its surface. Combining elegant design with cutting-edge technology, the EduTable transforms learning experiences by seamlessly integrating digital content into the physical environment.

Our product consists of different components including a monitor and a RFID-scanner fabricated by us. We do also offer the service of installing a complete setup with everything included.

If you feel satisfied with what we can provide you, then consider purchasing EduTable and make important knowledge more accessible!
